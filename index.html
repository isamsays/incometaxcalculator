💡 Tax Saving Tips & Information

🏥 Medical Expenses

You can claim medical expenses up to PKR 100,000 or actual expenses, whichever is lower.

🎓 Education Expenses

Tuition fees for up to 2 children are deductible from taxable income.

💝 Donations

Donations to approved institutions are deductible up to 30% of taxable income.

🏠 House Rent

House rent is deductible up to 45% of salary or actual rent, whichever is lower.

⚡ Utility Bills

Utility bills up to PKR 100,000 annually are deductible for salaried individuals.

📱 Mobile & Internet

Mobile and internet expenses up to PKR 25,000 annually are tax deductible.
