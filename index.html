Understanding Pakistan's Income Tax Structure

Current Tax Slabs for 2024-25

Pakistan's income tax system operates on a progressive structure, meaning higher earners pay higher rates. The income tax pakistan calculator considers these current slabs:

  • PKR 0 - 600,000: 0% tax rate
  • PKR 600,001 - 1,200,000: 2.5% tax rate
  • PKR 1,200,001 - 2,400,000: 12.5% tax rate
  • PKR 2,400,001 - 3,600,000: 20% tax rate
  • PKR 3,600,001 - 6,000,000: 25% tax rate
  • Above PKR 6,000,000: 32.5% tax rate
